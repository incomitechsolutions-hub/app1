# KPIs

## SEO KPIs
- indexed pages
- organic sessions
- ranking coverage
- CTR by page type
- crawl errors

## Content Operations KPIs
- draft-to-publish time
- pages published per month
- validation failure rate
- stale content count

## Lead KPIs
- inquiry volume
- inquiry conversion rate
- lead volume by page type
- CRM sync success rate

## Technical KPIs
- response time
- queue failure rate
- error rate
- cache hit ratio where used
