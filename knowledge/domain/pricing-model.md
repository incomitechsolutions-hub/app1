# Pricing Model

## Status
Initial domain note for future commercial features.

## Current Assumption
The MVP is lead-driven, not checkout-driven.

## Future Pricing Patterns
- request pricing
- from-price
- per participant pricing
- private group pricing
- delivery-mode dependent pricing

## Guidance
Do not force a single flat price field if the business uses custom quotes.

Potential later fields:
- pricing_type
- base_price
- currency
- min_participants
- max_participants
- quote_required
