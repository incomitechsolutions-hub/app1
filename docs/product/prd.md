# Product Requirements Document

## Product Vision
Build a scalable training platform that acts as:
- structured course catalog
- editorial content platform
- SEO content engine
- lead generation platform
- future base for training operations

The system should allow structured training management while generating SEO-optimized landing pages that attract organic traffic and convert visitors into leads.

## Product Goals
### Business Goals
- increase organic search visibility
- generate qualified inbound leads
- support thousands of indexable pages
- enable efficient content operations
- prepare the platform for future commercial and operational workflows

### Product Goals
- structured course and content management
- scalable page generation
- strong internal linking
- efficient SEO metadata management
- clear editorial workflow
- maintainable admin backend

## Target Users
### Content Editors
Create and maintain courses, pages, FAQs, and content blocks.

### SEO Managers
Manage metadata, canonicals, redirects, internal linking, and topic clusters.

### Administrators
Manage roles, settings, workflows, and integrations.

### Visitors
Browse content, discover training offerings, and submit inquiries.

### Future Operational Users
Sales, trainers, coordinators, and delivery managers.

## Core Problems
- large training catalogs are hard to manage consistently
- SEO landing pages do not scale without structure
- content and SEO are often disconnected
- internal linking is inconsistent
- generic CMS systems do not fit the domain deeply enough
